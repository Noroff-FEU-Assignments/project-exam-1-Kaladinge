Home/About

For those who don’t remember what I said on the home page, I will be so kind as to say it again: «You like guitars you say?! This blog site if for you.» For those who are satisified with this description and want to explore the articles and other content, please do. Those who want a little more can read the next paragraph too.

Well, when I started this blog I wanted it to be for all guitar lovers out there. I want people to feel at home here whether it is learning how to play the guitar, reading news related articles about your favorite bands and/or reviews about guitars and gears. Also, for those of you who like a personal touch I frequently update the blog with posts about my experience since I first started learning playing the guitar.

I hope the content can help you whatever it is your goals are. Whether it is becoming a better guitar player, becoming a guitar tech expert or be the local band encyclopedia at social gatherings I think doing so will be easier in a inspired environment where it is possible to reach out to other enthusiasts.

Stay inspired and rock on!

Lars-Inge G. J.
